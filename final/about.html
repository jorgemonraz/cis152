Our Heritage & Philosophy

Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort. Decades ago, this beautiful stretch of the California coast was a hidden secret, known only to a few dedicated hikers and bird watchers. Today, we have carefully transformed it into a sanctuary for anyone looking to unplug, recharge, and rediscover the joy of genuine, in-person connection.

Our mission goes beyond simply offering a place to sleep. We champion the lost art of the face-to-face gathering. After spending your morning kayaking along the coastline or exploring our scenic trails, the experience continues back at camp.

Imagine waking up to the aroma of freshly brewed drip coffee, spending the day out in the wild, and returning to your cozy yurt as the sun goes down. Our luxury yurts provide the perfect setting to gather around, share stories, and challenge your family and friends to board games deep into the evening. At Pacific Trails Resort, you are not just booking a room; you are reserving a space to build lasting memories away from the screens.
